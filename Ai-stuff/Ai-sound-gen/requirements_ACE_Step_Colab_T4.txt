# Tesla T4 = compute capability 7.5 (Turing) with 16 GB VRAM.
# Colab ships CUDA 12.x drivers, NOT CUDA 13.1, so we CANNOT use the prebuilt

#   * Keep Colab's stock PyTorch (already CUDA-matched).
#   * Skip flash_attn (Turing is poorly supported by v2 wheels).
#   * Skip SageAttention 2 (requires SM >= 8.0, T4 is SM 7.5).
#   * Skip the custom xformers cu13.1 wheel; PyTorch SDP attention is used.
# The app falls back to PyTorch's built-in scaled-dot-product attention.
